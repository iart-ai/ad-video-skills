# Testimonial Video

Animate a written quote, review, or testimonial into a short, trustworthy social-proof clip. The craft is typographic, not flashy: a clean quote card where the words land in reading order, the key phrase gets emphasis exactly when it should, the stars fill to the real score, and the author signs off. One card design, driven by props, becomes a video for every review on the list. For marketers and brand teams turning customer reviews into social proof.

## When it activates

- "Make an animated testimonial" or "create a quote / review video."
- "Turn a customer testimonial into a graphic" or "animate a customer review."
- "Make a social proof video" or "add an animated star rating."
- "Turn a list of reviews into videos."

## Example prompts

- "Turn this customer review into an animated testimonial with a 4.5-star rating."
- "Make a 9:16 quote card with the key phrase highlighted and the author block signing off."
- "Take this CSV of reviews and render one brand-locked testimonial video per row."

## What's inside

- `SKILL.md` — trust before motion, the anatomy of a quote card, staggered line reveal, kinetic emphasis on the key phrase, fractional animated star ratings, the author block, and template × reviews batching.
- `references/quote-card.md` — a complete runnable Remotion `Testimonial` composition: staggered line reveal, key-phrase highlight sweep, fractional star fill, author block, theme object, and the 9:16 timing map, plus a dependency-free HTML/CSS variant.
- `references/batch-and-typography.md` — the review prop schema, CSV/JSON→props parsing and validation, quote typography craft (smart quotes, hanging punctuation, line splitting, orphans), star-rating accessibility, and the batch render script for N videos.
