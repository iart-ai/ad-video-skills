# Ad Creative Video

Build ONE motion-graphics ad template, then mass-produce variants from a data table — different hooks, offers, CTAs, and products — so a performance marketer can A/B test dozens of versions across placements and aspect ratios. The craft is making the template fully data-driven and message-matched, so 40 variants stay on-brand and only the tested variable changes. For performance marketers and growth teams running UGC-style and direct-response video ads.

## When it activates

- "Make a video ad" or "create an animated / performance / UGC-style ad."
- "Batch-produce ad creative variations" or "generate ad variants for A/B testing."
- "Swap headline / offer / CTA across many ad versions."
- "Export one ad in multiple aspect ratios for Meta / TikTok / Reels."

## Example prompts

- "Make a UGC-style video ad and generate 5 hook variants from this CSV."
- "Take the winning hook and test it against 3 CTAs, one variable at a time."
- "Export this ad in 9:16, 4:5, and 1:1 with the CTA out of the bottom UI zone."

## What's inside

- `SKILL.md` — the two rules that make variants worth running, ad anatomy and hook taxonomy, the data-driven template (everything is a prop), the 1 template × CSV = N variants pipeline, and multi-aspect export.
- `references/batch-ad-pipeline.md` — the full runnable Remotion ad template, the CSV→typed-props parser with validation, a one-variable test-matrix generator, the brand-lock theme object, and the batch + multi-aspect render script.
- `references/platform-specs.md` — exact pixel safe zones, durations, aspect ratios, and format limits for Meta Feed/Reels/Stories, TikTok In-Feed, and YouTube, plus a hook→CTA message-match worksheet.
